THE ELIZABETHAN LANGUAGE - "WHY DIDN'T HE USE PLAIN ENGLISH?"

  • The works of William Shakespeare are often dreaded by students because of the language he uses!
  • "Why didn't he use plain, simple English?" is a question often used by students new to the Works of Shakespeare!
  • The answer is, of course, that he did! But the Elizabethan language was different to ours!
  • There weren't so many words and people used a whole variety of ways to spell them as dictionaries weren't available! (There are at least 16 different Elizabethan spellings of Shakespeare!)
